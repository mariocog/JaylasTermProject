The Censorship of American Entertainment

Censorship most commonly appears in entertainment. Television and film are two of the most influential forms of entertainment in the United States and therefore have a large hand in influencing the common interest of the public. Because of this, movies and TV shows tend to be cracked-down-on the most by government regulation.
