The FCC

The Federal Communications Commision (FCC) is a government agency responsible for regulating interstate and international communications by radio, TV, wire, satellite, and cable. The FCC holds a lot of power over the airwaves, but it is illegal for them prevent broadcasts from any point of view; This alligns with the first ammendment. However, when it comes to the censorship of TV, indecent material and profane material are prohibited between 6 AM and 10 PM (when there is a high likelyhood of children watching).
